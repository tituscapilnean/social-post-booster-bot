### Post
Harvey just raised at $11B to scale agents across law firms. Read the announcement carefully. The pitch quietly stopped being a fine-tuned legal model and became an agent platform. That is the whole story of vertical AI in one funding round.

The pattern repeats in every vertical I look at. Hippocratic in healthcare, Bloomberg-GPT in finance, the army of co-pilot startups in security and ops. Each one originally bet that fine-tuning on a proprietary corpus was the moat. Each one is now competing against a frontier model that is one Claude Skill, one MCP server, and one well-tuned retrieval pipeline away from the same answer at lower cost. Harvey's $11B round is what it looks like when the smartest team in vertical AI accepts the new physics and re-pitches as an agent company.

When raw reasoning stops being the differentiator, the differentiator becomes what context the model sees, in what order, with what verification. Embedding quality. Reranker policy. Vector store recall. The ability to surface a citation trail an auditor will actually accept. MongoDB bought Voyage AI for a reason. Turbopuffer is pricing vector search like object storage for a reason. ColBERT-style late interaction is back in production pipelines for a reason. ML-JKU's modern Hopfield layers (github.com/ml-jku/hopfield-layers) are quietly being repurposed as associative-memory retrieval primitives that pull exact patterns out of noisy embeddings, the kind of precision step that closes the hallucination gap in regulated workflows. The reranker is the new model selection, and the retriever is becoming a learned component, not a SQL call.

People keep filing this under tooling. It belongs in product strategy. The teams that win the next 12-18 months are the ones who can route a query to the right expert model, retrieve the right evidence with bounded recall, and emit a verifiable trail when the agent commits. RAG without hallucination, with provenance, at gateway speed.

The window is short. Anthropic already shipped a memory tool, a Skills system that loads knowledge files on demand, and an Advisor that routes between Sonnet and Opus. OpenAI's File Search and Vector Stores keep getting better. Retrieval is exactly the surface the providers absorb once it stabilizes.

Build the moat one embedding deeper than the platform can swallow.
