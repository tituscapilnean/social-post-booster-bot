- Reasoning depth dropped up to 67%, thinking chains shrinking from ~2200 to under 700 characters
- Behavior shifted from deliberate planning to shortcut execution
- Increased errors, reversals, and premature task completion
- Follows Anthropic's familiar pre-release pattern (Opus 4.5 degraded before 4.6 launch)

**Mythos gap analysis:**

- Gap may be inflated: comparing fully unlocked Mythos against safety-throttled Opus 4.6
