Prediction markets as institutional financial tool. Heavily crypto/Bitcoin flavored — use only the agentic finance angle.

**ProCap Insights — Agentic Research Partnership with Kalshi**
Pompliano's ProCap Financial (Nasdaq: BRR) partnered with Kalshi (largest prediction market) to launch an institutional-grade research offering exclusively covering prediction markets. ProCap Insights is described as "the first agentic research offering in finance" — AI agents digging through Kalshi data to surface insights. Findings: Kalshi traders calling the bluff on Wall Street analysts and being right; significantly mispriced markets found on Kalshi vs. traditional exchanges; economic data confirmed on Kalshi days before legacy system reporting.

**Kalshi CEO Quote**
Tarek Mansour: "prediction markets turn uncertainty about real-world events into actionable signals. We're partnering with ProCap Financial to bring wisdom-of-the-crowds intelligence directly to financial research, so both retail and institutional investors can benefit."

**Thesis (Agentic-Relevant Signal)**
Combination of agentic AI with prediction market data as a new class of financial intelligence. Rather than traditional hedging, prediction markets allow isolated bets on individual data points (exact interest rate at next Fed meeting, whether recession happens, individual earnings data points like Tesla deliveries). Farmers cited as natural use case: prediction markets replacing hedging/insurance strategies. Kalshi doubling down on commodities for these cases.
