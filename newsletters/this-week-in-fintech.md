Analysis of AI-powered bots/assistants across fintech and payments platforms.

**Key data points:**
- Block's Moneybot (Cash App AI assistant): only 20% of users have access despite months since announcement. Managerbot (Square) still in beta.
- Toast IQ: widely released, 50%+ of 164,000 locations using it within 4 months
- Shopify Sidekick: "hundreds of thousands of merchants" using it for core business operations
- Intuit AI agents (across payments, accounting, finance, customer service, marketing, payroll): used by 3M+ customers, 85%+ repeat usage

**Thesis:**
- AI native startups may overwhelm incumbent commerce platforms with better/faster/cheaper software
- AI-powered fraud schemes may swamp current fraud systems
- Fintech/payments share prices suffering from bearish AI thesis
- "May the best bot win" -- early days, AI assistants should improve dramatically as companies iterate with proprietary data

**Also:** Chime launches premium tier (Chime+), Robinhood powers Trump Accounts, Mastercard explores selling Nets real-time payments unit
