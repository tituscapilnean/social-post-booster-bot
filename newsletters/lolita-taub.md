Book announcement and community invitation from Lolita Taub (Ganas Ventures). Not AI-agent focused.

**Book: "What Actually Matters: Startups, Capital, and the Rules Nobody Taught Us"**
Publish date: September 15, 2026. First draft done, beta readers in, editing in progress. 10 chapters across 5 roles: first-time founder, investor, ecosystem builder, angel, GP/LP. Core concept: "the Access Tax" — invisible overhead you pay when building without the networks, warm intros, or safety net. Each chapter opens with a question most people carry for years before anyone says it out loud.

**Questions the Book Addresses**
- Why does raising money actually help, and when does it speed up the wrong thing?
- How do I write a check I can stand behind, without pattern-matching on the wrong things?
- What do I give away, and what do I charge for, and how do I stop burning out on the difference?
