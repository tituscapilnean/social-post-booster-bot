Q2 2026 State of AI Tools Guide (paid content, limited preview).

**Key arguments:**
- "What's the best AI tool?" is the wrong question. Answer expires in weeks, assumes bottleneck is the model (it isn't), puts you in shopper mode instead of operator mode
- Better question: "Are you getting the most out of the model you're already using?"
- Two people using same model get wildly different output. The gap is the wiring, not the model.
- "The harness matters more than the model" is the principle separating power users from everyone else
- 5-level operator ladder: from chat window to architect
- Scheduled Tasks and Dispatch changing what "working with AI" means
